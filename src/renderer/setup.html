Your Report Email Template

Paste your pre-typed email below. Use these placeholders and the app will fill them in automatically:

{SHOWING_COUNT} — showings in the past 2 weeks
{MARKET_SHOWINGS} — avg showings per listing in the area
{DAYS_ON_MARKET} — your listing's DOM
{AVG_DOM_ACTIVE} — average DOM for active listings
{MAX_DOM_ACTIVE} — max DOM for active listings
{MIN_DOM_ACTIVE} — min DOM for active listings
{TOTAL_SHOWINGS} — total showings since listing went live
{LIST_DATE} — date the listing went live
{NEW_LISTINGS} — details on new listings since last report
{STATUS_CHANGES} — listings that went under contract
{AVG_DOM_CLOSED} — average DOM for closed listings
{AVG_SOLD_PRICE} — average sold price in the area
{SHOWING_FEEDBACK} — agent feedback from showings
{OPEN_HOUSE_NOTES} — open house feedback
{AGENT_NAME} — the lead agent's name (e.g. Jordan)

AI Vision Setup

This is what lets the app visually inspect listing photos and judge update levels — just like you do manually. It costs roughly $0.50–$1.00 per report. Follow the steps below to get set up (takes about 2 minutes).

Step-by-step: Create your OpenRouter account

1
Go to OpenRouter
Open your web browser and go to openrouter.ai
2
Create a free account
Click "Sign Up" in the top right. You can sign up with your Google account or email — whichever is easiest.
3
Add credits
Once logged in, click your profile icon (top right) then "Credits". Add $5 to start — that's enough for several reports. You can always add more later. It accepts any credit or debit card.
4
Create an API key
Click your profile icon again, then "Keys". Click "Create Key". Give it any name you like (e.g. "Market Reports"). A long code starting with sk-or-v1- will appear.
5
Copy and paste the key below
Click the copy button next to your new key, then paste it into the field below. You only need to do this once — the app saves it securely on your computer.
Your key is encrypted and stored only on this computer. It never leaves your machine.
How much does it cost? Each report analyzes about 10–20 listings worth of photos. That typically costs $0.50–$1.00 per report using the default model (Gemini 2.5 Flash). A $5 credit balance will last several reports.
